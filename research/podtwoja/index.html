Pod Twoją Obronę, for 25 part choir

Pod Twoją Obronę (2013) | for 25 solo voices | Recorded live in Gdansk
Conductor: Jan Łukaszewski

Pod Twoją Obronę was commissioned by the Polish National Chamber Choir Polski Chor Kameralny to mark the 80th birthday anniversary of composer Henryk Górecki.

This work matches the large, resonant acoustics of the Kościół św. Katarzyny in Gdansk (in which the piece was performed) to the detailed part writing which occurs within the piece. Unlike the works in the architexture series, which are written from an evidence base created through acoustic impulse-response analysis, Pod Twoją Obronę relies on straightforward dimensions and materials measurements of the venue (made remotely) to inform the rates at which textures overlap and momentary vocal clarity can be achieved.
